Privacy Policy – Last updated: June 14, 2026

Welcome to ShieldBreaker (the "App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains that the App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We have designed the game to be completely private and secure, respecting all users.

📡 No Data Collection

ShieldBreaker does not collect, use, save, or transfer any personal data. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact details
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, Android ID)
  • Location data (GPS, network‑based)
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Usage statistics, crash logs, or analytics

The App never communicates with any external servers. All game data (high score, music settings) is stored locally on your device using Android’s SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is used only to remember your best kill count and your music preference.

🎮 Game permissions

ShieldBreaker does not require any dangerous permissions (e.g., camera, microphone, contacts, storage, location). The only technical requirements are:

  • Fullscreen / touch input – to play the game
  • Audio playback – to play background music (optional, can be turned off in settings)

The App does not have Internet permission, so it cannot send or receive any data over the network.

🧸 Children’s privacy

Because no data is collected at all, ShieldBreaker is suitable for children of all ages. We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations – there is simply no personal information to protect. Parents can feel confident letting their children play without any tracking or hidden data collection.

🔊 Music & Audio

The App includes background music that is stored locally in the app bundle (background_music.mp3). No audio is recorded, transmitted, or shared. You can disable music at any time from the in‑game Settings menu. Your preference is saved locally.

🔗 Third‑party services

ShieldBreaker does not integrate any third‑party SDKs (advertising, analytics, social media, or crash reporting). There are no ads, no in‑app purchases, and no external links. Therefore, no third party receives any information from your use of the game.

📂 Local storage (SharedPreferences)

The following information is stored only on your device and is never accessible to us or any other party:

  • Your highest kill score (bestScore)
  • Your music on/off preference (musicEnabled)

You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the App or by clearing the App’s storage from your device settings.

🔄 Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Since the App does not collect data, changes will only clarify or improve the policy. Continued use of the App after modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
