🎾 Privacy Policy

Zigzag Ball  •  Effective Date: 06/06/2026

Zigzag Ball is a simple one‑tap arcade game where you guide a ball along a zigzagging track. No accounts. No tracking. Just pure reflex‑based fun.

🔒 This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything runs entirely on your device.

Information Collection and Use

Zigzag Ball does not collect any personal or user data.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No gameplay data is sent to any server
  • No tracking or analytics tools are used
  • No internet connection is required to play

Local Gameplay Data

The app stores minimal data locally on your device, including:

  • Your best (high) score – saved using Android SharedPreferences

This data is stored only on your device and is never shared externally. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Gameplay

Tap anywhere on the screen to change the ball’s diagonal direction. Stay on the green zigzag track to keep playing. The longer you survive, the higher your score. The track changes colour every 50 points, and the ball’s speed gradually increases.

App Permissions

Zigzag Ball requires no special permissions. It does not access your storage, location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any other sensitive data.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use advertisements, analytics, or any third‑party services. No external libraries that collect data are included.

Children’s Privacy

Since no personal data is collected, Zigzag Ball is safe for users of all ages and fully complies with privacy‑friendly regulations such as GDPR and COPPA.

Data Security

With no data collection, there is no risk of data misuse, leaks, or unauthorised access.

Data Retention

Only your high score is stored locally. Uninstalling the app removes this data completely.

🎾 Simple truth: You tap. The ball zigzags. Nothing leaves your device.

Data Deletion

You can clear all stored data by uninstalling the app, which deletes your high score instantly. Alternatively, you can clear the app’s data via Android Settings.
