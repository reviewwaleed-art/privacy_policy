Privacy Policy – Last updated: June 13, 2026

Welcome to Tunnel Escape (the "Game"), developed by Tunnel Escape Studio ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you play our game on Android devices.

🛡️ TL;DR: Tunnel Escape does not collect, store, or share any personal data. No accounts, no trackers, no analytics – just pure arcade fun.

1. Information we collect

None. Tunnel Escape is designed to be completely offline and private. We do not:

  • ❌ Collect your name, email, or any contact details.
  • ❌ Track your device ID, IP address, or location.
  • ❌ Use cookies or web beacons.
  • ❌ Integrate third-party analytics (Firebase, Google Analytics, etc.).
  • ❌ Display any third-party ads that could collect data.

The game does not require an internet connection, and all game data (high scores) are saved locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your phone.

2. Local high scores

To remember your best performance, we store your highest score on your device's internal storage. This is not accessible by us or any other app. You can clear the game's data at any time from your device's settings, which will reset your high score.

3. Children's privacy

Our game is safe for all ages. Since we collect no personal information, there is no risk of unknowingly gathering data from children under 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

4. Permissions

Tunnel Escape requires zero special permissions. It does not access camera, microphone, contacts, storage (except for its own private app data), or any sensitive sensors. The only permission used is the default INTERNET if you ever download an update, but the game itself works fully offline. No internet connection is needed during gameplay.

5. Third-party services

The game uses no third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools. Everything runs locally.

6. Data security

Because we don't collect any data, there is nothing that could be lost, stolen, or misused. Your privacy is fully protected by design.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We recommend checking occasionally, but since no data is ever collected, updates will only clarify our non‑collection stance.
