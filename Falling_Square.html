🟦 Privacy Policy

Falling Square  •  Effective Date: 10/06/2026

Falling Square is a fast‑paced arcade game where you slide a cute square left and right to dodge falling obstacles. Collect power‑ups like Invincible, Slow, Double Points, and Super Speed to survive longer and beat your high score. No accounts. No tracking. Everything runs entirely on your device.

🔒 This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. No internet connection is required to play.

Information Collection and Use

Falling Square does not collect any personal or user data.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No gameplay data is sent to any server
  • No tracking or analytics tools are used
  • The app works completely offline – no internet permission required

Local Gameplay Data

The app stores minimal data locally on your device, including:

  • Your best (high) score – saved using Android SharedPreferences

This data is stored only on your device and is never shared externally. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Gameplay

Drag your finger anywhere on the screen to move the square horizontally. Avoid falling coloured blocks – each block that safely passes below you gives +1 point. Collect special falling power‑ups:

  • 🛡 Invincible – ignore collisions for 4 seconds
  • 🐢 Slow – reduces obstacle speed by half for 5 seconds
  • 💰 Double Points – each passed obstacle gives 2 points for 6 seconds
  • ⚡ Super Speed – obstacles fall 10× faster for 2 seconds while you stay invincible – huge point rush!

The game becomes faster every 5 points, increasing obstacle speed and reducing spawn delay. How long can you survive?

App Permissions

Falling Square requires no special permissions. It does not access your storage, location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any other sensitive data. The app may request vibration permission, but only for optional haptic feedback on collisions and power‑up collection. This permission is not required to play.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use advertisements, analytics, or any third‑party services. No external libraries that collect data are included.

Children’s Privacy

Since no personal data is collected, Falling Square is safe for users of all ages and fully complies with privacy‑friendly regulations such as GDPR and COPPA.

Data Security

With no data collection, there is no risk of data misuse, leaks, or unauthorised access.

Data Retention

Only your high score is stored locally. Uninstalling the app removes this data completely. You can also manually clear the app’s data via Android Settings.

🎮 Simple truth: You slide. You dodge. You collect power‑ups. Nothing leaves your device.

Data Deletion

You can clear all stored data by uninstalling the app, which deletes your high score instantly. Alternatively, go to Android Settings → Apps → Falling Square → Storage → Clear Data.
