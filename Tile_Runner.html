Privacy Policy • Effective: June 6, 2026

Tap. Jump. Conquer the endless tower of tiles.
No tracking. No accounts. Pure arcade fun.

🔒 Zero data collection promise – TileRunner does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Everything stays on your device, always.

📱 Information Collection

TileRunner does not collect any personal or user data. No names, emails, device IDs, or location. We don't even know you're playing.

  • ✅ No personal information requested or stored
  • ✅ No gameplay telemetry sent to any server
  • ✅ No analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs
  • ✅ No internet permission – the game works fully offline

🎮 Local Data (on your device only)

The only thing TileRunner ever saves is your high score and preferences (like sound on/off). This data lives exclusively inside your phone's private app storage using Android's SharedPreferences.

  • Local Best score
  • Local Audio setting (mute/unmute)

Uninstalling the app wipes this data instantly. No backup, no cloud, no sharing.

⚙️ Required Permissions

TileRunner asks for zero permissions. It never accesses:

  • ❌ Storage / Photos / Files
  • ❌ Location (GPS)
  • ❌ Camera or Microphone
  • ❌ Contacts or Phone state

Only basic display & touch input – that's it.

🔊 Audio & Media

Background music uses a local MP3 file (bundled inside the app). No streaming, no internet requests. The game respects Android audio focus, so your music or calls will pause the game audio appropriately.

👶 Children's Privacy

Because we collect absolutely no data, TileRunner is safe for all ages. It fully complies with GDPR (EU), COPPA (US), and other global child privacy laws.

🛡️ Data Security

No data collection means no risk of data breaches, leaks, or unauthorised access. Your privacy is built‑in by design.

🎯 Simple truth: You tap a tile → the ball jumps → your score increases. Nothing else. No spies. No servers. No tricks.

🧹 Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by TileRunner, simply:

  • Uninstall the app from your device, OR
  • Go to Android Settings → Apps → TileRunner → Storage → Clear Data

That's it. Your high score disappears forever.

📅 Changes to this Policy

If TileRunner ever adds new features that affect privacy (unlikely, but possible), this page will be updated with a new effective date. We'll never sneak in data collection without telling you.
