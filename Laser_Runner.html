Privacy Policy • Effective: June 10, 2026

Swipe. Jump. Dodge the lasers.
No tracking. No accounts. Pure neon arcade action.

🔒 Zero data collection promise – Laser Runner does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Everything stays on your device, always.

📱 Information Collection

Laser Runner does not collect any personal or user data. No names, emails, device IDs, or location. We don't even know you're playing.

  • ✅ No personal information requested or stored
  • ✅ No gameplay telemetry sent to any server
  • ✅ No analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs
  • ✅ No internet permission – the game works fully offline

🎮 Local Data (on your device only)

The only thing Laser Runner ever saves is your high score and preferences (like sound on/off). This data lives exclusively inside your phone's private app storage using Android's SharedPreferences.

  • Local Best score (high score)
  • Local Audio setting (mute/unmute)

Uninstalling the app wipes this data instantly. No backup, no cloud, no sharing.

⚙️ Required Permissions

Laser Runner asks for zero permissions. It never accesses:

  • ❌ Storage / Photos / Files
  • ❌ Location (GPS)
  • ❌ Camera or Microphone
  • ❌ Contacts or Phone state

🔊 Audio & Media

Sound effects (if any) are generated locally or played from bundled assets. No streaming, no internet requests. The game respects Android audio focus, so your music or calls will pause the game audio appropriately.

👶 Children's Privacy

Because we collect absolutely no data, Laser Runner is safe for all ages. It fully complies with GDPR (EU), COPPA (US), and other global child privacy laws.

🛡️ Data Security

No data collection means no risk of data breaches, leaks, or unauthorised access. Your privacy is built‑in by design.

⚡ Simple truth: You swipe to change lanes → tap to jump → avoid red lasers. Nothing else. No spies. No servers. No tricks.

🧹 Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by Laser Runner, simply:

  • Uninstall the app from your device, OR
  • Go to Android Settings → Apps → Laser Runner → Storage → Clear Data

That's it. Your high score disappears forever.

📅 Changes to this Policy

If Laser Runner ever adds new features that affect privacy (unlikely, but possible), this page will be updated with a new effective date. We'll never sneak in data collection without telling you.
